Database

All our data is kept in firestore realtime NoSQL database and therefore the application needs to be constantly connected to the internet. Changes made in the database are therefore instantly observed within our application. The application also stores all its images within the Cloudimage which will ensure the best optimisation and fast delivery of the images.for more information about cloudimage visit. here All student details are made available only to the hostel manager incharge.

Security

All users will need to be authenticated before they can access the app either through email and password or google signin. Firebase Authentication uses the data to enable end-user authentication, and facilitate end-user account management. It also uses user-agent strings and IP addresses to provide added security and prevent abuse during sign-up and authentication. Managers will have to surbsribe to the application which will ensure only authentic individuals are given access to the system data. for more information on firebase security visit here

Hostel Online is available to every campus student looking for a hostel to book without having to waste alot of time checking out all the differnt rooms in the hostels physically.The app helps you narrow down your choices of rooms making it easier for room selection and location with the maps services. Hostel managers are free to subscribe to the app and add their hostel rooms if they are looking for a place to not only advertize their free rooms but also keep track of the students in their rooms and the payments made.
