  • Student User:
    • Launch the app.
    • Sign up with either your email and password or using your google account.
    • Select a list of Qualities that are of interest to you and hit the enter button as you wait for the list of rooms the app assigns to you.
    • select the one of the rooms provided that has caught your interest.
    • view information about hostel policies and rooms such as wifi,visiting hours,and others.
    • book the room

The students needs to signup with the course name,real names,a profile picture which is optional and their phone number. These details are available to the hostel manager incase the student books a room.

  • For The Hostel Manager
    • Launch the app
    • Sign up
    • add information about the hostel
    • add rooms to hostel account
    • make rooms available
    • add students to rooms
    • receive payments from students
    • view list of students in rooms and their information

The hostel manager can always edit the details about the rooms and the students staying in these rooms for easier management. He can always pass on information to the students in the rooms through the app.
