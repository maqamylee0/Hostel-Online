    • select the one of the rooms provided that has caught your interest.
    • view information about hostel policies and rooms such as wifi,visiting hours,and others.

  • The student can view the notifications such as payments due dates from the hostel manager as well as any other announcements by visiting the notifications page.
  • The student can also make payments as well as receive confirmations and see outstanding balance.
  • Please Take note that you can originally sign up as a student and not as a manager.

  • The admins are the only ones who can make managers able to add hostels after they pay a subscription flex-item
    • Launch the app
    • Sign up

  • The hostel manager can edit the details about the rooms and the students staying in these rooms for easier management.
  • He can pass on information to the students in the rooms through the app notifications page.
  • They are also able to capture the location of their hostels using the capture location button.
  • The images for single,double and tripple rooms are also added as the manager adds a hostel.
  • The manager can always view the students in his rooms as well as send them notifications such as payments due dates as well as any other announcements.
  • The rooms can be added and the type easily set by clicking single to enable the red color that adds a single room, the double to enable the purple color that adds double rooms and tripple to enable the green color that adds tripple rooms.
